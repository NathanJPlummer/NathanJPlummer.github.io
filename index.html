UNC Qualtrics Faculty Survey

Project link

Qualtrics Survey

Using Qualtrics I developed this survey to collect data during the self-study. Qualtrics provided us with a couple of important, immediate advantages. For one, we could tailor the survey to the users' early responses, modifying the questions displayed in real time so faculty wouldn't have to sort through information not relevant to them on an individual level (example: asking questions about PhD students if the faculty member only advised MPH students). Additionally, we were able to quickly develop infographics based on the responses, and even have those infographics automatically update as we recieved additional or even modified data. A complex report that may have taken days- or even weeks- was generated in just under 4 hours.
