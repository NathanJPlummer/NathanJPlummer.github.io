Kimberly

The original Pink Ranger, Kimberly Hart was active during the wars against Rita Repulsa and her husband Lord Zed. Kimberly is capable gymnast and notable for her unique martial arts style, which combines traditional defensive manuevers with US style gymnastics.

After nearly three years as a Ranger, Kimberly left the team to pursue gymnastics full time. She gave her power coin to Katherine Hillard who became the new Pink Ranger.
