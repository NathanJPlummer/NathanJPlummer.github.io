Mighty Facts

    In the Beggining

    The Original "Power Rangers" series premiered on August 28th, 1993. The series was created by combining newly shot American footage with clips of the Japenese Television series "Zyuranger". The series was so popular the American producers ran out of footage. The original series creates were hired to created new footage exclusive to the American version.

    Multimedia

    Power Rangers has become a multimedia sucess. Besides three feature films, the Power Rangers have spun-off into multiple comic book series and over a dozen video games. The current comic series involve the Rangers teaming up with Warner brothers/DC Entertainment's "Justice League."

    Power Rangers: The Next Generation

    A new Power Rangers film series hit theatrs on March 24th, grossing over forty million in its first weekend.
