Irrigation of Crops with Recycled Wastewater: a Remediation Technology or Safe for Consumption

Student Researcher: Ramon (DC) Alatorre, BS

This proposal will investigate the use of green infrastructure design for the removal of targeted chemical and biological pollutant indicators from domestic wastewater and the potential presence of these indicators in the edible portions of consumptive crops grown with such wastewater treated in this manner. The project goal will be to demonstrate whether more widespread use of constructed wetlands could be part of a managed approach to pollutant remediation and/or crop irrigation thereby saving freshwater for more critical needs.
