Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I Organize My Modern Developer Coursework Repository?

    For your first few assignments assignments a Modern Developer team member will create the project folders for you. Simply follow the folder structure already put in place.

    If you need further context, take a look at the Modern Developer Example Student Repository.

    Take note that the Example Student Repository is a private GitHub page. You will have to be logged in to GitHub with the same account you use for Modern Developer in order to view it.

  2. How soon after completing a course can I expect feedback? What type of feedback will I receive? Can I continue on to the next course before I receive feedback?

    Feedback Priority

    This is an area being continually reevaluated and changed. For now this is how the feedback system works:

    • Completing certain courses will trigger an automatic unlock of the following course within minutes.
    • Other courses, which we feel will strongly benefit from feedback on previous work, will remain locked until a Modern Developer instructor has reviewed your work and deemed it satisfactory for you to move forward.
    • Timing is variable. We prioritize feedback on courses that require feedback before a course unlock so students do not spend more time than necessary waiting for the next course.

    Requesting a Course Unlock

    If you’ve been able to move on to the next course then please be patient, as feedback can take up to a couple weeks.

    However, if you are waiting on a course unlock, and more than 48 hours have passed and you have still not received feedback, please e-mail issues@moderndeveloper.com with a quick explanation. In most cases, you’ll receive access to the next course in a few hours.

    Feedback Structure

    Feedback instructors may point out some specifics, but the focus will be on general areas that could use some improvements or a different approach. Work we find to be outstanding or to show excellence will also be commented upon. Here’s an example of student feedback from the course “Introduction to CSS.”

    Where to Find Your Feedback

    Feedback will be provided via your Modern Developer registered e-mail address for only the first few courses. Once we get you up to speed on GitHub, feedback will hence be given using GitHub’s “Issues” feature.

  3. Why does Modern Developer not unlock all student courses from the start of the program?

    Learn.Modern Developer doesn’t function like a Massive Open Online Course or an online course repository site where you can take any course whenever and keep chugging along. Over 94% of students do not complete such courses and programs.

    Modern Developer is designed to allow you, the student, to set frequent small goals, and let your ambition be your perseverance.

    We have a specific system and we believe it is to your benefit
